About Association

The students of CSE established Department-level Student Technical Association in the year 2000 as Computer Engineers Technical Association (CETA). The Association aims to provide a good platform for the students to improve their talent and provides exposure to Outside world. It encourages the students to Organize and participate in various Technical, non-technical, co-curricular and Extracurricular Activities.

Vision

To serve as a robust forum for well rounded personalities of students

Mission

  • Expansion of knowledge of students by exposing them to intra inter and multi disciplinary knowledge areas with an emphasis on self-learning.
  • Development of comprehensive skill set among students through activities and events for enhancing their career prospects in Industry and Research.
  • Provide opportunities for students to put into practice their knowledge and skills to develop orientation for need assessment and application.
  • Inculcate professional skills among students for career progression and life- long learning.

Objectives

  • To serve as a unique forum for knowledge building in Novel areas of domain and allied areas.
  • To promote multi dimensional skill development among students for employability and research demands.
  • To provide a platform for expression of knowledge and skills for feasible applications upon identification of needs of society, industry and research.
  • To offer congenial environment for inculcating team spirit professional behaviour and ethical attitude.
